a. There will be five questions in Buzzer Round.
b. The first team to signal the buzzer will answer the question in five seconds after being acknowledged by the moderator. Failure to answer will cost 10 marks for the same team.
c. Answer that any team gives without the signal of a buzzer or light will cost 10 marks for that team.
d. 20 marks will be awarded for each correct answer.
e. It is a non-transferable round. So the questions that are not answered correctly by the teams will not be passed to other teams.
f. If two teams signal the buzzer at the same time, they are asked to submit the answer in the paper and are judged accordingly.
g. Each team has to press the bell or light to answer the questions within 30 seconds.
